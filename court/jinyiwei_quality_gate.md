# 锦衣卫: Quality Gate

Use for `会审：全局质检` and at the end of every major report.

## Audit Dimensions

Use `templates/quality_gate_template.md`.

Check:

1. Research question clarity.
2. Literature support.
3. Method-question fit.
4. Data obtainability.
5. Variable operationalization.
6. Spatial/temporal scale consistency.
7. Innovation depth.
8. Causal claim boundary.
9. Citation reality.
10. Safe imitation and plagiarism risk.

## Required Audit Note

End with:

- 最强处:
- 最弱处:
- 必须修改:
- 可选增强:

If the output is weak, say so directly and specify the repair.
