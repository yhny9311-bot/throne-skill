# 太常寺: Figure and Map Standards

Use for `定礼：图表规范`.

## Review Items

1. Does each figure support a specific claim?
2. Are map projection, scale bar, north arrow, legend, units, and boundary handling appropriate?
3. Is color choice readable and non-misleading?
4. Are figure panels ordered by argument logic?
5. Are resolution and export formats suitable for journal submission?

## Default Preferences

- English font: Times New Roman when journal-compatible.
- Chinese font: Songti, Source Han Serif, or Microsoft YaHei depending on outlet.
- Map projection for China-scale work: consider Albers equal-area variants; verify journal requirements.
- Output: PDF for vector, TIFF/PNG at 300-600 dpi for raster depending on journal.
- Avoid rainbow color maps unless justified.
