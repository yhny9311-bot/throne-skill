# 尚宝司: Citation and Source Seal

Use for `用印：引文核查`.

## Iron Law

No citation enters a formal draft unless it has at least one real anchor: uploaded paper, DOI, Crossref/Semantic Scholar/arXiv/database record, Zotero entry, or user-provided bibliographic file.

## Required Outputs

1. Citation inventory.
2. Status for each citation: verified, missing metadata, suspicious, unsupported claim.
3. DOI/BibTeX fields when available from real source material.
4. Claims without support.
5. Replacement search strategy if support is missing.

## Risk Signs

- Perfect-sounding title but no DOI or database trace.
- Journal, volume, issue, and page mismatch.
- Citation supports a different claim than the sentence says.
- AI-generated paragraph contains many citations not present in user materials.
