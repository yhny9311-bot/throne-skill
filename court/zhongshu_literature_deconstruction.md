# 中书省: Literature Deconstruction

Use for `上朝：文献解构`.

## Required Outputs

1. Material status: list papers/files actually available, unreadable items, and missing context.
2. Literature cards: one card per paper using `templates/literature_card_template.md`.
3. Cross-paper method matrix using `templates/method_matrix_template.md`.
4. Transferable method components: what can be borrowed, under what conditions.
5. Research gaps: variable, scale, mechanism, data, method, and writing gaps.
6. 3-5 research pathways. Each must include title, scientific question, theoretical lens, method chain, expected data, innovation claim, feasibility, and main risk.
7. `锦衣卫按语`.

## Method

- Extract the full method chain: data acquisition -> preprocessing -> indicator construction -> modeling -> validation -> interpretation.
- Translate every method into reusable components, not just a summary.
- Use `references/landscape_method_library.md` to classify the paper into landscape research paradigms.
- Distinguish author-claimed innovation from your own gap analysis.
- If only abstracts are available, mark conclusions as abstract-level and avoid detailed method claims.

## Pathway Quality Bar

A pathway is weak if it only combines fashionable methods. It is stronger when it has:

- a clear landscape or urban ecological problem;
- a measurable dependent variable;
- obtainable data;
- a method that answers the question;
- a plausible mechanism;
- a non-trivial contrast with existing papers.
